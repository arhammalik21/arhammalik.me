DaySavvy Startup Overview

Startup: DaySavvy

Founder: Arham Malik

DaySavvy is an emotionally intelligent AI assistant designed to help students and professionals manage daily workload without cognitive overload. It converts unstructured input from text, voice, and images into structured plans, priorities, and reminders.

Problem

Traditional productivity tools demand manual input and rigid workflows. Users often struggle with scattered tasks, poor consistency, and low mental bandwidth.

Solution

DaySavvy acts as a conversational planning layer that extracts actionable tasks, prioritizes intelligently, and adapts to user context with empathetic responses.

Key Features

  • AI-powered action extraction from text and images
  • Conversational task management interface
  • Smart scheduling and prioritization
  • Emotion-aware responses and support
  • Voice interaction with real-time assistance
  • Integrated calendar and reminders
